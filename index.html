Formula Guide

Electric Charge

Q = ne

Symbols

  • Q = Charge (Coulombs)
  • n = Number of electrons
  • e = 1.6 × 10⁻¹⁹ C

Steps

  1. Identify number of electrons.
  2. Multiply by electron charge.
  3. Get total electric charge.

Coulomb's Law

F = kq₁q₂ / r²

Symbols

  • F = Electric Force (N)
  • k = 9 × 10⁹
  • q₁ = First charge
  • q₂ = Second charge
  • r = Distance

Steps

  1. Multiply q₁ and q₂.
  2. Multiply by constant k.
  3. Square the distance.
  4. Divide numerator by denominator.

Electric Field

E = F / q

Symbols

  • E = Electric Field (N/C)
  • F = Force
  • q = Charge

Steps

  1. Identify force.
  2. Identify charge.
  3. Divide force by charge.

Ohm's Law

V = IR

Symbols

  • V = Voltage (V)
  • I = Current (A)
  • R = Resistance (Ω)

Steps

  1. Identify current.
  2. Identify resistance.
  3. Multiply I and R.

Electric Power

P = VI

Symbols

  • P = Power (Watts)
  • V = Voltage
  • I = Current

Steps

  1. Identify voltage.
  2. Identify current.
  3. Multiply V and I.

Resistance

R = V / I

Symbols

  • R = Resistance
  • V = Voltage
  • I = Current

Steps

  1. Identify voltage.
  2. Identify current.
  3. Divide V by I.

Magnetic Force

F = BIL sin θ

Symbols

  • F = Magnetic Force
  • B = Magnetic Field
  • I = Current
  • L = Length
  • θ = Angle

Steps

  1. Multiply B, I, and L.
  2. Get sin θ.
  3. Multiply all values.
